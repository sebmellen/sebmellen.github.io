The Story of the Festival

     The idea for the Brooklyn Outdoor Film Festival began a few years ago, in a small coffeshop on a Brooklyn street corner, as a few friends talked together about the epidemic of high-class art never reaching the proletariat masses.  After many years of careful consideration and planning, last year we finally ran our first festival. This was a very exciting thing for us - a moment we'd been looking forward to for years. However, while the festival went swimmingly, we only had 200 attendees, and would like to expand our audience this time around.

     That's why, this year, we are inviting 5,000 people to come to the Brooklyn Bridge Park and watch some of the best independent works of the year with us. We're planning to show everything from award winning documentaries to short, thought inspiring films, and will supplement the experience with voluntary group discussions on the topics presented. Check our social media and "news" page for announcements and our "showings" page for the films you can see at the festival. If you have any suggestions, send us a message on one of our social media pages, which are linked below, or send us an email with the contact form. We look forward to seeing you soon :).
